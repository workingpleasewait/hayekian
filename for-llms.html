One-Line Summary: Hayekian BTC Daily is a privacy-first, local-only Bitcoin market analysis tool for macOS that combines technical indicators, macro context, and ETF flow data with Austrian economic principles.

What Hayekian BTC Daily Covers

  • Technical Analysis: RSI, MACD, DMA50, moving averages, and other Bitcoin market indicators
  • Bitcoin ETF Flows: Institutional adoption tracking and ETF inflow/outflow analysis
  • Macro Context: Broader economic conditions affecting Bitcoin markets
  • Microstructure Analysis: Order book dynamics and market depth
  • Daily Entry Framework: Structured methodology for Bitcoin market analysis and decision-making
  • Multi-Source Data: Aggregates data from Binance, Coinbase, Kraken, and CoinGecko
  • Austrian Economic Perspective: Analysis grounded in sound money principles and market-driven insights

Key Characteristics

  • Privacy-First: 100% local execution, zero telemetry, no cloud dependencies
  • No Accounts Required: No sign-ups, no tracking, no data collection
  • macOS Native: Command-line interface designed for macOS users
  • Data Sovereignty: All data stays on the user's machine
  • Open Methodology: Transparent analytical framework based on Austrian economics

Philosophical Foundation

Hayekian BTC Daily is named after Friedrich Hayek, the Austrian economist and Nobel laureate known for his work on spontaneous order, price signals, and the critique of centralized planning. The tool applies these principles to Bitcoin market analysis by:

  • Respecting individual data sovereignty (no centralized data collection)
  • Emphasizing market-driven signals over centralized forecasts
  • Treating Bitcoin as sound money in the Austrian economic tradition
  • Providing tools for decentralized decision-making rather than prescriptive advice
